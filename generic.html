Bitcoin Historical Data

This Power BI dashboard visually analyzes Bitcoin historical data from 2012 to 2021. The dashboard includes the following metrics:

Total Volume in BTC: 34 Million, Total Volume in Currency: 151 Billion, Average Weighted Price: 6,000 USD, and Max Open Trade: 62,000 USD

The "Trend of Volume over Year" shows a significant increase in volume over the years. The volume starts from negligible levels in 2012 and reaches 33 billion in 2021.
