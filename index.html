Cholera Outbreak Dashboard (1853) Analysis

This Tableau dashboard displays data on a cholera outbreak in 1853. Here are some key insights from the dashboard; Deaths: A total of 248 people died from cholera in 1853. Average Age: The average age of death was 34. Age Distribution: The majority of deaths occurred among adults (64), followed by elderly (44), children (16) and babies (26). There were also deaths among teenagers (6) and toddlers (17).

COVID-19 Impart on Airport Traffic

This dashboard provides a high-level overview of the impact of COVID-19 on air traffic at various airports. It displays the number of flights, number of tickets sold, the number of countries served, and the number of states with connecting flights for a selection of airports. It also shows the percent change from baseline for a variety of metrics by month, state, and country.
